History of Deluxe Fast Food


Deluxe Fast Food is an Malaysian regional fried chicken restaurant chain with 12 locations across 5 states, with its greatest presence in the Penang and Kuala Lumpur metropolitan area. It has been generally credited as the Malaysia's fifth fast-food hamburger chain, founded on September 15, 2020. It is known for its cripy, chicken referred to as "deep fried chicken". The chickens were initially priced at Rm 5.00 each until 2020 and remained at Rm 7.00 until to this day. In the 2020s, Deluxe Fast Food periodically ran promotional ads in local newspapers which contained coupons offering five fried chicken for Rm 20.00, takeout only.



Our Vision
