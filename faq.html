FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

When is the operating hours for Deluxe Fast Food?

Deluxe Fast Food is available from 10.00am to 10.30pm from Mondays to Sundays including public

holidays.

Do you offer the same menu options for Foodpanda and Grab Food as in the restaurants?

Except for some items that are inappropriate for delivery, most of our menu is available.

What do I do if my order arrived with missing items??

Please call 1300-13-6900 and inform our customer service personnel. We will then arrange

for the items to be delivered to you.

Is the restaurants accessible?

The restaurant building is accessible from the street to wheelchair users. Unfortunately,

due to the listed status of the building, we are currently unable to offer any dedicated

disabled toilets.

Are you hiring?

We are always looking for hospitality professionals who are hard working and want to learn.

Please send us your CV to careers@deluxefastfood.co.my if you are interested in joining

the team!
